CA1 - Week 06 (worth 15% of final module mark) :

  • The OS1 2025 CA1 is a closed-book individual-based in-lab Brightspace Quiz Assessment. It will consist of a mixture of multiple choice questions, fill-in-the-blank questions and matching questions.  There will be no negative marking.  The lecturer will provide a demonstration during the lecture on Week 04 of the types of fill-in-the-blank questions and matching questions that may appear in the CA1.
     
  • For the duration of the CA1, students are only permitted to access Brightspace (to sit the CA1 only, not to reference lecture/lab materials) and access the Google Cloud Shell.  Access to all other resources (physical or digital) are prohibited (students are not allowed to access previously created documents or files either in physical form or in their Google Cloud Shell account or any where else).

  • When and where: The CA1 takes place on Wednesday 5th March 2025 during your normal scheduled lab session.  There is only one sitting of the CA1 and this is it!  Attendance is mandatory.  There will be no resit opportunities. 

  • Student must attend the lab session for which they are officially scheduled according the lab schedule made available on Brightspace under "Preparation for Lab Activities" of this module.

  • The CA1 will begin at exactly 5 minutes past the hour when the lab session commences (for example: 12:05pm or 1:05pm).  It will have a total duration of 45 minutes.  It will start on time and finish on time, regardless of whether students arrive late (or not at all).  It will automatically submit the student quiz at 12:50pm or 1:50pm if the student has not already submitted it.  If a student arrives late or has problems logging on, that is on them - there is no opportunity to extend the quiz for the student.  Students may leave the room when they have completed and submitted the CA1.  It is the student's responsibility to ensure they have correctly submitted their CA1. 

  • Toilet breaks are not permitted during the CA1, because the duration of the CA1 is only 45 minutes.

  • All students must put their phones on silent and then place them away in their bags after successfully sign-in to Brightspace. No phones/pens/paper/etc are permitted on the desks.  No USB keys/drives may be plugged into the desktops/laptops.

  • The CA1 will consist of two components:
    • A Lecture assessment component worth 6% of final module mark. It will consist of 8 questions (each question worth 0.75 marks) . It will have a duration of 15 minutes.
    • A Lab assessment component worth 9% of final module mark.  It will consist of 12 questions (each question worth 0.75 marks). It will have a duration of 30 minutes.

  • What is examinable in CA1?
    • All lectures and materials from week 01 to week 05 inclusive.
    • All lab materials and activities from week 02 and week 05 inclusive.
    • All additional reading materials from week 01 to week 05 inclusive.

  • The CA1 must be completed on the Desktop PCs in the lab.  Only those student who cannot access a desktop because none are physically available must seek the explicit permission of the Teaching Assistant to sit the CA1 using their own laptop, before they are allowed to do so.

  • For the duration of the CA1, the Internet browser is the only application on the desktop/laptop permitted to be running or active. Within the browser application, only two tabs may be open: one for Brightspace and one for the Google Cloud Shell.  Students are only permitted to access Brightspace to sit the CA1, not to access lecture/lab materials or any other resources. Access to all other resources (physical or digital) are prohibited (students are not allowed to access previously created documents or files either in physical form or in their Google Cloud Shell account or anywhere else).

  • No pens/papers/notes may be on the desk during the CA1. No cheating/copying/plagiarism or AI assistance of any kind is permitted.
  • If you have any questions concerning the CA1, please ask the lecturer during the lecture so all students may benefit from hearing the answer.
